1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how Digital Solutions uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website (ds.barros.no). It describes what these technologies are, why we use them, and your rights to control them.

This policy should be read alongside our Privacy Policy, which explains how we handle personal data in general.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They allow the site to remember your actions and preferences over time, making your browsing experience more efficient and personalized.

First-Party Cookies

Set directly by our website. Used for core functionality, security, and remembering your language/consent preferences.

Third-Party Cookies

Set by external services (like analytics providers). Used to understand how users interact with our site and improve performance.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Strictly Necessary Cookies

Always Active

Essential for the website to function properly. They enable core features like page navigation, secure access to forms, and load balancing.

  • • Session identifier cookies
  • • Security/CSRF protection cookies
  • • Consent preference storage

Analytics & Performance Cookies

Optional

Collect anonymized data about how visitors use our site. This helps us understand which pages are most popular, how users navigate, and where improvements are needed.

  • • Page view counters
  • • Bounce rate tracking
  • • Traffic source identification

Marketing & Functional Cookies

Optional

Used to deliver relevant content and remember your preferences (like language or region). May also be used to limit ad frequency or measure campaign effectiveness.

  • • Language/region preferences
  • • Video player settings
  • • Social sharing integration

4. How We Use Cookie Data

Data collected through cookies is used exclusively for:

  • Ensuring website security and functionality
  • Analyzing traffic patterns to improve user experience
  • Remembering your preferences and consent choices
  • Optimizing content delivery and page load speeds

Note: We do not use cookies to identify you personally or to track your behavior across unrelated websites.

5. Third-Party Cookies

We may use trusted third-party services that set their own cookies. These providers are contractually obligated to use data only for the services we request.

Google Analytics

Anonymized traffic analysis and performance monitoring. Data is aggregated and stripped of personal identifiers.

Cloudflare / Security

DDoS protection, bot management, and security threat analysis. No personal profiling is performed.

You can manage or block third-party cookies through your browser settings or our consent banner.

6. Managing Your Preferences

You have full control over non-essential cookies. You can change your preferences at any time using the methods below:

Cookie Consent Banner

Click the cookie icon at the bottom of the page to reopen the consent panel and adjust your choices.

Browser Settings

Most browsers allow you to view, delete, or block cookies. Note that blocking all cookies may affect website functionality.

Private/Incognito Mode

Browsing in private mode prevents cookies from being stored after you close the browser window.

7. Cookie Retention Periods

Different cookies have different lifespans. We minimize retention to only what is necessary:

Cookie Type Duration Purpose
Session Until browser closes Security & navigation
Consent Preference 12 months Remember your choices
Analytics 14 months Traffic analysis
Functional 6 months Language & region

8. Your Rights

Under GDPR and Norwegian law, you have the right to:

  • Withdraw consent for non-essential cookies at any time
  • Request deletion of cookie data associated with your device
  • Object to analytics tracking
  • Lodge a complaint with Datatilsynet (Norwegian Data Protection Authority)

To exercise these rights, contact us at post@barros.no. We respond within 30 days.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically to reflect changes in technology, legislation, or our business practices. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will indicate when changes were made. We encourage you to review this policy regularly.
